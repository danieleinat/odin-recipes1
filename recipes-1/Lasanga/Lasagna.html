Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved layered pasta dish, packed with rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese. It's a crowd-pleaser that's perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or any special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles until tender.
  2. Brown the beef and add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until bubbly and golden.
