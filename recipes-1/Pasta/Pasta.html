Pasta

Description

This classic pasta dish is a simple yet hearty meal that's perfect for any occasion. Rich sauces and al dente noodles come together to create a comforting, satisfying plate. It's an ideal recipe for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends when you want something delicious without too much effort.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Spaghetti or Penne)
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Combine pasta with the sauce and top with cheese.
